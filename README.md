# I Made an App for NeXTSTEP,
## or how I learned to stop worrying and love Objective-C
Thank you for coming to my Try! Swift Tokyo 2025 lightning talk. While the talk was short, I hope it inspired your curiosity. I would love to do a longer talk on this subject, but lets be real, its a Swift conference, not a 30 year old Objective-C conference.
